# Commercial Use

This is a plain-language guide. The controlling terms are in [`../LICENSE`](../LICENSE).

## Personal And Non-Commercial Use

The Gauntlet is free for personal, educational, academic, hobby, and other
non-commercial use under the Motion-TimeSpace Non-Commercial License.

## Commercial Use

Commercial use requires a separate signed written commercial license from the
copyright holder/company.

Commercial use can include, for example:

- using The Gauntlet inside a paid product or service
- using The Gauntlet for client work, consulting, or paid research
- using The Gauntlet inside a company, startup, or commercial R&D workflow
- offering The Gauntlet outputs as part of a paid report, audit, or platform

For commercial licensing discussions, contact the company's COO or licensing
contact.

## Third-Party Papers And Result Packs

The Gauntlet license covers this software, documentation, tests, and included
metadata-only manifests. It does not grant rights to third-party papers, PDFs,
datasets, or downloaded source files.

Result-pack manifests should remain metadata-only: titles, expected filenames,
source links, notes, and license/access notes. Do not bundle paper PDFs or copied
paper text unless you have the right to redistribute them.

## AI Providers

The normal checker is local-first and non-AI. Optional Gemini/OpenAI/Anthropic
refinement remains separate and requires the optional dependency path plus
session-only API keys. Provider terms and API costs are separate from The
Gauntlet license.
